Redefining Royalty, Luxury and Comfort, Maharajas' express takes you on a sojourn to the era of bygone stately splendour of princely states. Sylvan furnishings, elegant ambience and modern amenities are amalgamated for an “Experience Unsurpassed”. It has been a winner of “World’s Leading Luxury train” by World Travel Awards.

IRCTC operates Bharat Gaurav Tourist Train having AC III-Tier accommodation on train specially designed to promote domestic tourism in India.
